About Us

A Legacy of Mining Excellence

Established in 1990, Unimines has grown into a premier global trading and investment house. We underwent strategic restructuring in 2011 to facilitate our expanding operations across continents.

Our expertise spans ferrous & non-ferrous raw material trading, trade finance, ocean transportation, vessel ownership, logistics, property, and mining investments. With offices in Hong Kong, Dubai, and Singapore, we serve clients across 50+ countries.
